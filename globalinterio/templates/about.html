ABOUT US

Global Interiors is a full-service residential interior design and build firm, founded by Amit Shadija in 2013 at Ahmednagar city.

Through his years of experience, Amit has developed a unique style of mixing old with new and giving each project a timeless feel.

Seamlessly juxtaposing limestone, marble, earthy woods, textured fabrics and organic colors with materials such as metal and glass is what gives Amit's work a striking and harmonious feel.

Global Interiors' philosophy is to create inviting and elegant interiors straying from trends that will only disappoint in years to come.

Our design approach is to Architecturally space plan every square inch of a room to work both aesthetically and functionally. Each project is tailored specifically to each client, as Amit pays special attention to every detail that goes into project.

Global Interiors Design is committed to using sustainable materials, wherever possible.
